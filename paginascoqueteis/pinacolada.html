PINA COLADA

Ingredient Unit Amount
White Rum ML 40
Coconut Liqueur ML 20
Coconut Puree ML 20
Pineapple Juice ML 90
Pineapple Slice U 1
Marrachino Cherry U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Shaker

Glass: hurricane

International Day: 10 JUL

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into shaker filled with ice. Shake briskly for few seconds. Strain into chilled hurricane glass.
Garnish:Pineapple slice & marrachino cherry

STORYTELLING

The Pina Colada was created in Puerto Rico in 1954 by bartender Ramón Marrero at the Caribe Hilton Hotel. Marrero combined rum, coconut cream, and pineapple juice to craft a drink that embodied the tropical spirit of Puerto Rico. The Piña Colada quickly became popular and was even named Puerto Rico's official drink in 1978.
