TOMMY'S MARGARITA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Tequila ML 30
Fresh Lime Juice ML 30
Agave Syrup ML 30
Lime Wheel U 1
Salt GR 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Shaker

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a ice filled old fashion glass.
Garnish: Half glass rimmed with salt & lime wheel

STORYTELLING

The Tommy’s Margarita was created in the early 1990s by bartender Tommy Cordero at Tommy’s Mexican Restaurant in San Francisco. Looking to simplify the classic Margarita, Cordero swapped the orange liqueur for agave nectar, allowing the natural flavors of the tequila to shine. The drink quickly gained popularity for its clean, bold taste and became a modern twist on the traditional Margarita. Today, the Tommy’s Margarita is a beloved variation, celebrated for its purity and emphasis on high-quality ingredients.
