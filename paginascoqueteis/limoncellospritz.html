LIMONCELLO SPRITZ

Ingredient Unit Amount
Limoncello ML 45
Prosecco ML 85
Soda Water ML 30
Lemon Juice ML 20
Lemon Slice/Wheel U 1
Mint Bouquet U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice U 1

Method: Built

Glass: Wine or Spritz

International Day: 22 JUN

PREPARATION

Build all ingredients into a wine glass filled with ice. Stir gently.
Garnish: 2 Lemon slice or 1 lemon wheel & mint bouquet

STORYTELLING

Limoncello is a popular Italian lemon liqueur, mainly from southern Italy. Made by infusing lemon zest in alcohol, it has a sweet, tangy flavor. Typically served chilled as a digestif after meals, it's known for its bright, refreshing taste.
