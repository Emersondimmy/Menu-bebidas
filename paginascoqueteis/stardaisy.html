STAR DAISY

Ingredient Unit Amount
Gin ML 20
Brandy Mel ML 20
Fresh Lemon Juice ML 20
Triple-Sec Liqueur ML 20
Lemon Zest U 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Coupe or Cocktail

PREPARATION

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a chilled coupe glass.
Garnish: Lemon Zest

STORYTELLING

The Star Daisy is a relatively modern cocktail that has gained popularity in recent years. While its exact origin is unclear, it is known for its refreshing and vibrant flavors, often combining citrus with a touch of herbal or floral notes. The drink has become popular in craft cocktail bars, offering a light, balanced taste that makes it a great choice for those seeking something both elegant and easy-drinking. The Star Daisy is often associated with a contemporary twist on classic cocktails, appealing to both seasoned cocktail enthusiasts and newcomers alike.
