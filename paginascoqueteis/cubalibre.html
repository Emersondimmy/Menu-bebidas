CUBA LIBRE

Ingredient Unit Amount
White Rum ML 50
Coca Cola ML 120
Fresh Lime Juice ML 10
Lime Wedge or Lime Wheel U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

Build all ingredients in a highball glass filled with ice.
Garnish: Lime wheel or lime wedge.

STORYTELLING

The Cuba Libre is a classic cocktail with a simple, refreshing flavor profile. It's essentially a variation of the Rum and Coke, but with the addition of lime juice, which gives it an extra layer of tartness and depth. The name "Cuba Libre" translates to "Free Cuba," and it is said to have originated in Cuba around the time of the Spanish-American War (late 19th century).
