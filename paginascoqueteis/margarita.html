MARGARITA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Tequila ML 50
Triple-sec ML 20
Fresh Lime Juice ML 15
Salt GR 1
Lime wheel U 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Coupe or Cocktail

International Day: 22 FEB

PREPARATION

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
Garnish: Half salt rimmed glass & lime wheel

STORYTELLING

The Margarita is a timeless cocktail with a debated origin. One popular story credits its creation to bartender Carlos Herrera in the 1930s at his bar in Tijuana, Mexico, made for a woman named Margarita. Another tale ties it to Margaret Sames, an American socialite, who is said to have invented it for her guests in Acapulco in the 1940s. Regardless of its true roots, the Margarita quickly became a beloved drink, celebrated for its perfect balance of tequila, lime, and orange liqueur. It remains one of the most iconic cocktails worldwide, synonymous with sunny days and festive gatherings.
