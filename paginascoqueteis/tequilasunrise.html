TEQUILA SUNRISE

Ingredient Unit Amount
Tequila ML 45
Fresh Orange Juice ML 90
Grenadine Syrup ML 15
Orange Wheel U 1
Marrochino Cherry U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Hurricane or Highball

PREPARATION

Pour tequila and orange juice directly into highball glass filled with ice cubes. Add the grenadine syrup to create chromatic effect (sunrise), do not stir.
Garnish: Orange wheel & marrachino cheery

STORYTELLING

The Tequila Sunrise was created in the 1970s in California, likely at the Trident Bar in Sausalito. It gained widespread fame after being associated with the Rolling Stones during their 1972 tour. The cocktail’s vibrant, layered appearance mimics the colors of a sunrise, making it as visually striking as it is flavorful. Over time, it became a symbol of laid-back beach culture, often enjoyed in tropical settings and at parties. The Tequila Sunrise remains a classic, known for its refreshing taste and beautiful presentation.
