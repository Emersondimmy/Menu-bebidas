ESPRESSO MARTINI

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vodka ML 50
Coffee Liqueur ML 30
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 10
Espresso U 1
Coffee Beans U 3

Method: Shaker

Glass: Cocktail

International Day: 15 MAR

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker, shake well with ice, strain into chilled cocktail glass.
Garnish: 3 coffee beans.

STORYTELLING

The Espresso Martini was created in the 1980s by bartender Dick Bradsell at Fred's Club in London. The story goes that a famous model asked for a drink that would "wake me up and then f*** me up." Inspired by her request, Bradsell combined vodka, fresh espresso, and coffee liqueur, resulting in a bold, energizing cocktail. The drink quickly became a hit, and today the Espresso Martini remains a trendy favorite for coffee lovers and cocktail enthusiasts alike.
