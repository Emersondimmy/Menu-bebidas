AMARETTO SOUR

Ingredient Unit Amount
Amaretto ML 45
Fresh Limon Juice ML 25
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 25
Vegan Foam ML 1
Lemon Wheel U 1
Marrachino Cherry U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Shaker

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well. Strain into old fashion glass filled with ice. If requested, serve chilled in a sour glass.
Garnish: Lemon wheel & marrachino cherry.

STORYTELLING

Amaretto is a sweet Italian liqueur with a distinct almond flavor, though it’s often made with apricot pits or almonds. It has a rich, smooth taste, with hints of vanilla and a slight bitterness. Amaretto is typically enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in cocktails like the Amaretto Sour. It originated in Saronno,Italy, and is a popular choice for both sipping and mixing.
