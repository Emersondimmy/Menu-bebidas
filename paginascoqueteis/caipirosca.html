CAIPIROSCA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vodka ML 60
White Sugar GR 20
Lime Wedges U 3
Straw U 1
Lime Wheel U 1
Cracked Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Place lime and sugar into old fashioned glass and muddle gently. Fill the glass with cracked ice and add white vodka.Stir gently.
Garnish: Lime Wheel.
