MOSCOW MULE

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vodka ML 45
Fresh Lime Juice ML 10
Ginger beer ML 90
Lime wheel / Wedge U 1
Mint Bouquet U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed ice & Crushed

Method: Built

Glass: Mule cup

International Day: 3 MAR

PREPARATION

In an Mule Cup, combine the vodka and ginger beer. Add lime juice and gently stir to involve all ingredients. Top with cracked ice.
Garnish: Lime wheel/wedge & mint bouquet

STORYTELLING

The Moscow Mule was created in the 1940s at the Cock 'n' Bull pub in Hollywood. It was the result of a collaboration between a vodka distributor and a ginger beer maker, both trying to popularize their products. The drink quickly gained popularity, partly thanks to its distinctive presentation in a copper mug. Despite its name, the Moscow Mule has no real ties to Russia but became a hit for its refreshing, zesty flavor and remains a beloved cocktail today.
