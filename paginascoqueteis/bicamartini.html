BICA MARTINI

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vodka ML 30
Bicabagaço ML 20
Tijuana Liqueur ML 20
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 10
Espresso U 1
Topping Salted Caramel ML 5
Coffee Bean U 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Coupe

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker, shake well with ice, strain into chilled cocktail glass.
Garnish: Make a flower with the salted caramel and put the coffee bean in the middle.
