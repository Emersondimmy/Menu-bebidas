FRENCH 75

Ingredient Unit Amount
Prosecco ML 60
Gin ML 30
Fresh Lime Juice ML 20
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 20
Lime Wheel U 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Flute

PREPARATION

Pour all the ingredients, except prosecco, into a shaker. Shake well and strain into a flute glass. Top up with prosecco.
Garnish: Lemon zest.

STORYTELLING

The French 75 is a classic cocktail that dates back to World War I and is named after the French 75mm field gun, a powerful weapon used during the war, symbolizing the drink's strength and impact. The cocktail combines gin, champagne, lemon juice, and sugar, creating a refreshing, bubbly, and slightly tart drink with a hint of botanical flavor from the gin. It became widely popular in the 1920s, especially in Parisian bars, and has since become a timeless choice for those seeking an elegant and sparkling drink. The French 75 is perfect for celebrating special occasions, offering both the sophistication of champagne and the crispness of gin.
