MIMOSA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Fresh Orange Juice ML 75
Prosecco ML 75
Orange Zest U 1

Method: Built

Glass: Flute

PREPARATION

Pour orange juice into flute glass and gently pour the sparkling wine. Stir gently.
Garnish: Orange Zest

STORYTELLING

The drink’s origins are often debated, but it is believed to have been created in the 1920s at the Café Parisien in Paris, France. It was initially called the "Buck’s Fizz," after the Buck’s Club in London, where a similar drink was served. The Mimosa gained popularity in the U.S. in the 1960s, particularly as a go-to cocktail for brunches and celebrations like weddings and holidays.
