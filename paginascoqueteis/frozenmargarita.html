FROZEN MARGARITA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Tequila ML 40
Triple-sec liqueur ML 15
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 15
Fresh Lime Juice ML 20
Flavoured Puree ML 30
Salt GR 1
Garnish U 1
Straw U 1

Method: Blend

Glass: Margarita or Cocktail

PREPARATION

All ingredients with a scoop of crushed ice. Fine: strain into a chilled glass.
Garnish: Half rimmed glass & fruit according to the puree flavour
REPLACE THE PUREE WITH FRESH FRUIT IF YOU HAVE IT
