OLD FASHIONED

Ingredient Unit Amount
White Sugar or Cube Sugar GR 20
Whisky ML 50
Agostura Bitter ML 1
Orange Zest ou Slice U 1
Plain Water ML 1
Marrachino Cherry U 1
Ice Cubes or Ice Block

Method: Built

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Place sugar cube and saturate with bitter, add few dashes of plain water. Muddle until dissolved. Fill the glass with ice cubes and add whiskey. Stir.
Garnish: Orange zest or slice and a marrachino cherry

STORYTELLING

The cocktail dates back to the early 19th century, and its name comes from the "old- fashioned" way it was originally made. It was first created at the Pendennis Club in Louisville, Kentucky, in the 1880s, though variations of it had been around for years before. Over time, the Old Fashioned became a staple in bars across the world, particularly gaining popularity in the mid-20th century. The Old Fashioned is often associated with classic cocktail culture and remains a favorite for those who enjoy strong, spirit-forward drinks. Its balanced, bittersweet flavor and rich history make it a go-to choice for cocktail enthusiasts.
