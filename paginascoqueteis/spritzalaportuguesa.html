SPRITZ Á LA PORTUGUESA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Orange Liqueur ML 50
Prosecco ML 90
Tonic Water ML 30
Orange Slice U 2
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball or Spritz

PREPARATION

Build all ingredients into a highball glass filled with ice.
Garnish: 2 orange slices

STORYTELLING

The orange liqueur from the Algarve, "Licor de Laranja", is a traditional Portuguese liqueur made from locally grown oranges, particularly the Bitter Seville oranges. This liqueur is a product of the Algarve region, where oranges are abundant, and it often combines the fresh, tangy flavor of the citrus fruit with sugar and alcohol. Licor de Laranja is typically sweet and aromatic, with a slightly bitter edge from the Seville oranges. It is enjoyed as an after-dinner drink or used in cocktails. The Algarve's sunny climate and rich agricultural tradition contribute to the liqueur's distinct, vibrant flavor, making it a unique regional specialty.
