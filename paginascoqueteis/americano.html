AMERICANO

Ingredient Unit Amount
Aperitif Bitter ML 30
Sweet Red Vermouth ML 30
Soda Water ML 20
Orange Slice U 1
Lemon Zest U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Mix the ingredients directly in an old fashioned glass filled with ice cubes. Add a splash of Soda Water. Stir gently.
Garnish: Half orange slice and lemon zest.

STORYTELLING

The Americano cocktail dates back to the late 1800s, originally known as the "Milano-Torino" due to its ingredients: sweet vermouth from Turin and bitters from Milan. It was later renamed to appeal to American tourists in Italy, offering a refreshing blend of Campari, sweet vermouth, and soda water.
