BICICLETA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Campari ML 40
White Wine ML 40
Soda Water ML 40
Orange Wheel U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients directly into chilled highball glass filled with ice. Stir gently.
Garnish: Orange Wheel.

STORYTELLING

The Bicicleta is a classic Italian cocktail that emerged in the 20th century. It is believed to have been created in Italy, and its name, meaning "bicycle," reflects the simple, refreshing nature of the drink, much like a leisurely bike ride. The Bicicleta became popular for its light, crisp flavors, making it a go-to choice for outdoor gatherings or as a pre-dinner drink. Its simple yet delicious combination of ingredients has kept it a beloved, easy-to-make option in bars around the world.
