DAIQUIRI

Ingredient Unit Amount
White Rum ML 60
Fresh Lime Juice ML 20
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 10
Lime Wheel U 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Cocktail

International Day: 19 JUL

PREPARATION

In a cocktail shaker add all ingredients. Add ice and shake. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.
Garnish: Lime Wheel.

STORYTELLING

The Daiquiri originated in the late 19th century in the Cuban town of Daiquiri, where American mining engineer Jennings Cox is credited with creating the drink by mixing rum, lime, and sugar. The cocktail gained popularity after bartender Constantino Ribalaigua refined it at La Floridita in Havana, where it became a favorite of American tourists, including Ernest Hemingway. Over time, the Daiquiri evolved into variations like the Frozen Daiquiri and fruity versions, solidifying its place as a timeless, classic cocktail.
