PALOMA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Tequila ML 50
Grappefruit Soda ML 90
Fresh Lime Juice ML 15
Grappefruit Slice U 1
Salt GR 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

Poor the tequila into a highball glass, squeeze the lime juice. Add ice, fill up pink grapefruit soda. Stir gently.
Garnish: Wheel slice of lime & half salt rimmed glass

STORYTELLING

The Paloma cocktail is a refreshing Mexican classic, born in the 1950s in Tequila, Jalisco. Legend has it that bartender Don Javier Delgado mixed tequila with grapefruit soda, creating a perfect balance of sweetness, bitterness, and the warmth of tequila. Named after the Spanish word for "dove," the Paloma has become a symbol of summer, offering a crisp, citrusy drink that's light yet full of flavor. Simple, refreshing, and beloved worldwide, it's the perfect choice for any occasion.
