BRAMBLE

Ingredient Unit Amount
Gin Comapnhia Portuguesa Limão ML 40
Fresh Limen Juice ML 30
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 10
Chambord liqueur ML 20
Orange Flower Water(2 drops) ML 0.10
Lemon Wheel U 1
Blackberry U 2
Straw U 1
Cracked Ice

Method: Shaker

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker except the blackberrys liqueur, shake well with ice, strain into chilled old fashioned glass filled with crushed ice, then pour the blackberry liqueur (Crème de Mûre) over the top of the drink, in a circular motion.
Garnish: Lemon wheel & 2 blackberrys.

STORYTELLING

The Bramble cocktail was created in 1984 by bartender Dick Bradsell at Fred's Club in London. It was inspired by classic gin-based drinks but incorporated a fruity twist with blackberry liqueur. The cocktail became popular for its refreshing, berry-forward flavor and vibrant appearance, typically served over crushed ice and garnished with fresh blackberries. Over time, the Bramble has become a modern classic, enjoyed in bars worldwide.
