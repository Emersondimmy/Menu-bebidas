COSMOPOLITAN

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vodka Citron ML 40
Triple-Sec Liqueur ML 15
Fresh Lime Juice ML 15
Cramberry Juice ML 30
Lemon Zest U 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Cocktail

International day: 7 May

PREPARATION

Add all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well and strain into large cocktail glass.
Garnish: Lemon zest.

STORYTELLING

The Cosmopolitan, often simply called the "Cosmo," is a popular cocktail that gained widespread fame, particularly in the late 1990s and early 2000s, thanks to its association with the Sex and the City TV series. It's known for its stylish appearance, light pink color, and crisp, refreshing flavor.
