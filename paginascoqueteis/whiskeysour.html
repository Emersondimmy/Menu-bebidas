WHISKEY SOUR

Ingredient Unit Amount
Whiskey ML 45
Fresh Lemon Juice ML 25
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 20
Vegan Foam ML 1
Orange Zest U 1
Marrachino Cherry U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Shaker

Glass: Old Fashion

International Day: 25 AUG

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well. Strain into old fashion glass filled with ice. If requested, serve chilled in a sour glass.
Garnish: Orange zest & marrachino cherry

STORYTELLING

The Whiskey Sour dates back to the mid-1800s, with some crediting its creation to a bartender named Evan Williams in the 1860s. It quickly became a popular drink, known for its perfect balance of sour and sweet flavors. The cocktail's simplicity and smoothness helped it endure as a classic, and it remains a staple in bars around the world, influencing many other sour-style cocktails over time.
