DAIQUIRI MADEIRENSE

Ingredient Unit Amount
William Hinton Rum ML 45
Fresh Lime Juice ML 30
Banana Syrup ML 15
Lime Wheel U 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Cocktail or Coupe

PREPARATION

In a cocktail shaker add all ingredients. Add ice and shake. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.
Garnish: Lime Wheel.

STORYTELLING

Ilha da Madeira, often referred to simply as Madeira, is a stunning Portuguese archipelago located in the Atlantic Ocean, southwest of Portugal. Known for its rugged terrain, lush landscapes, and subtropical climate, it is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The island boasts dramatic cliffs, volcanic peaks, and rich flora, making it a haven for hikers. Madeira is also famous for its wine, botanical gardens, and vibrant culture, offering a unique blend of Portuguese heritage and natural beauty.
