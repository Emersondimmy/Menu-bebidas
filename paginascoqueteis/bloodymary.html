BLOODY MARY

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vodka ML 45
Tomato Juice ML 50
Fresh Lemon Juice ML 15
Tabasco ML 0.5
Worcestershire Souce ML 0.5
Celery Salt GR 1
Pepper GR 1
Lemon Wedge UN 1
Celery Stick UN 1
Straw UN 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball or Old fashion (No Ice)

International Day: 1 JAN

PREPARATION

Stir gently all the ingredients in a mixing glass with ice, pour into rocks glass. If requested served with ice, pour into highball glass.
Garnish: Celery, lemon wedge.

STORYTELLING

The Bloody Mary is believed to have been created in the 1920s by bartender Fernand Petiot at Harry’s New York Bar in Paris. Initially a simple mix of vodka and tomato juice, it became a popular brunch cocktail, known for its savory flavors and unique combinations of spices, garnishes, and the possibility of being tailored to individual tastes.
