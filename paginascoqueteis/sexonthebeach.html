SEX ON THE BEACH

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vodka ML 40
Peach Schnapps Liqueur ML 20
Fresh Orange Juice ML 40
Cramberry Juice ML 40
Orange Wheel U 1
Marrachino Cherry U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Hurricane or Highball

PREPARATION

Build all ingredients in a highball glass filled with ice.
Garnish: Orange wheel & marrachino cherry

STORYTELLING

The *Sex on the Beach* cocktail was likely created in the 1980s, with one popular story attributing its invention to bartender Ted Pizio in Florida in 1987. He crafted the drink for college students during spring break, using vodka, peach schnapps, cranberry juice, and orange juice. The catchy name helped it become a beach party favorite, and it has since become a classic cocktail associated with fun, summer, and relaxation.
