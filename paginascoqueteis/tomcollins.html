TOM COLLINS

Ingredient Unit Amount
Old Tom Gin ML 45
Fresh Lemon Juice ML 30
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 15
Soda Water ML 60
Lemon Wheel / Wedge U 1
Marrachino Cherry U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients directly into highball filled with ice. Stir gently.
Garnish: Lemon wheel/wedge & marrachino cheery

STORYTELLING

The Collins cocktail dates back to the 19th century, linked to a bartender named John Collins at Lloyd’s Cocktail Bar in London. The drink became famous through a hoax about a "Tom Collins," and the cocktail evolved into various versions using gin, whiskey, or vodka. Today, it's a classic, known for its refreshing, fizzy flavor.
