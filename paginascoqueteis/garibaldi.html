GARIBALDI

Ingredient Unit Amount
Aperitif Bitter ML 45
Fresh Orange Juice ML 120
Orange Wedge GR 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

Build all ingredients in a highball glass filled with ice.
Garnish: Orange Wedge

STORYTELLING

The Garibaldi cocktail, named after Italian hero Giuseppe Garibaldi, blends Campari and fresh orange juice, reflecting the vibrant colors of the Italian flag. While its exact origins are unclear, it emerged in Italy in the mid-20th century as a simple, refreshing drink that balanced the bitterness of Campari with the sweetness of orange juice. Today, the Garibaldi remains a popular, easy-to-make cocktail, celebrated for its bold flavor and Italian roots.
