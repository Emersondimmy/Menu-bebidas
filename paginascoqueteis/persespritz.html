PER SE SPRITZ

Ingredient Unit Amount
Per Se Aperitif ML 60
Prosecco ML 30
Tonic Water ML 90
Orange Slice U 2
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Wine or Spritz

PREPARATION

Build all ingredients into a wine glass filled with ice. Stir gently
Garnish: 2 orange Slices

STORYTELLING

Perse Aperitif is a new Portuguese aperitif made with a mix of local botanicals, fruits, and herbs, including orange and citrus, giving it a refreshing and slightly herbal flavor profile. It’s typically served chilled, either neat or with tonic, and is designed to be a light, easy-drinking beverage enjoyed as an aperitif before meals. Reflects the flavors of Portugal’s landscape, with a focus on natural ingredients, and has gained popularity for its fresh, versatile taste.
