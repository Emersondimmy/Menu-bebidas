AMARGUINHA SOUR

Ingredient Unit Amount
Amarguinha liqueur ML 45
Fresh Limon Juice ML 25
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 20
Vegan Foam ML 1
Lemon Wheel U 1
Marrachino Cherry U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Shaker

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well. Strain into old fashion glass filled with ice. If requested, serve chilled in a sour glass.
Garnish: Lemon wheel & marrachino cherry.

STORYTELLING

Amarguinha is a Portuguese liqueur made from bitter almonds, sugar, and alcohol. It has a sweet yet slightly bitter taste and is often enjoyed as a digestif. Originating in the Algarve region, it's typically served chilled or over ice and is popular in Portugal and beyond.
