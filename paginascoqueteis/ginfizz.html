GIN FIZZ

Ingredient Unit Amount
Gin ML 45
Fresh Lime Juice ML 30
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 10
Soda Water ML 30
Lemon Slice/Wheel U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Shaker

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

Shake all ingredients with ice except soda water. Pour into thin tall Tumbler glass , top with a splash soda water.
Garnish: Lemon Slice or Lemon Wheel

STORYTELLING

The Gin Fizz is a classic cocktail that dates back to the late 19th century. It was likely first created in the U.S. as a variation of the "fizz" family of drinks, which combined citrus, spirits, and carbonation. The Gin Fizz became popular for its light, refreshing taste, with gin, lemon juice, sugar, and club soda. Its effervescent nature and balance of tart and sweet flavors made it a favorite in bars and still remains a timeless cocktail, especially enjoyed in warmer months.
