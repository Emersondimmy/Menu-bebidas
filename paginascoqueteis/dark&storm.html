DARK & STORM

Ingredient Unit Amount
Dark Rum ML 60
Ginger Beer ML 100
Lime Wheel U 1
Straw U 1

Method: Built

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

In a highball glass filled with ice pour the ginger beer and top floating with the Rum.
Garnish: Lime Wheel.

STORYTELLING

The Dark 'n' Stormy is a classic cocktail that originates from Bermuda. It is made with dark rum (typically Gosling’s Black Seal rum), ginger beer, and a splash of lime juice. The drink is known for its bold, spicy flavor from the ginger beer, balanced by the rich sweetness of the dark rum. It’s traditionally served over ice in a highball glass, garnished with a lime wedge, and is beloved for its refreshing yet warming qualities. The Dark 'n' Stormy is often considered the national drink of Bermuda and is perfect for those who enjoy a tangy, spiced cocktail.
