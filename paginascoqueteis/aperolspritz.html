APEROL SPRITZ

Ingredient Unit Amount
Aperol ML 60
Prosecco ML 90
Soda Water ML 30
Orange Slice U 2
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Large Wine

PREPARATION

Build all ingredients into a wine glass filled with ice. Stir gently
Garnish: 2 Orange slices.

STORYTELLING

The Aperol Spritz originated in Veneto, Italy, in the early 20th century. Initially, it was a lighter version of wine diluted with water, a practice started by the Austrian occupiers. Over time, the drink evolved and became a popular aperitivo in Italy. Its bright, refreshing nature made it a go-to for outdoor gatherings and casual socializing. The Aperol Spritz gained international fame, embodying the carefree, sun-soaked lifestyle of Italy.
