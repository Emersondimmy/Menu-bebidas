KIR ROYAL

Ingredient Unit Amount
Prosecco ML 120
Creme Cassis ML 15
Blackberry U 2

Method: Built

Glass: Flute

PREPARATION

Pour Crème de Cassis into glass, top up with prosecco.
Garnish: 2 Blackberry

STORYTELLING

The Kir Royal is a French cocktail made with champagne and crème de cassis (a blackcurrant liqueur). It’s a variation of the classic Kir, which uses white wine instead of champagne. The drink is named after Félix Kir, a French priest and mayor of Dijon, who popularized the combination of crème de cassis and wine in the 1940s. The Kir Royal became a favorite for celebrations due to its elegant and bubbly nature. The rich, fruity flavor of the cassis complements the dry champagne, making it a sophisticated, festive drink.
