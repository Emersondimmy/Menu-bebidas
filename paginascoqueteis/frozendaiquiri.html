FROZEN DAIQUIRI

Ingredient Unit Amount
White Rum ML 50
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 10
Fresh Lime Juice ML 15
Flavoured Puree ML 30
Garnish U 1
Straw U 1

Method: Blend

Glass: Margarita or Cocktail

PREPARATION

Blend all ingredients with a scoop of crushed ice. Fine strain into a chilled glass.
Garnish: According to the flavour pure
REPLACE THE PUREE WITH FRESH FRUIT IF YOU HAVE IT & PUT MORE SUGAR SYRUP

STORYTELLING

The Frozen Daiquiri is a modern twist on the classic Daiquiri, which was created in Cuba in the early 1900s. In the 1980s, bartenders blended the original cocktail with ice, turning it into a refreshing, slushy drink perfect for hot days. The Frozen Daiquiri quickly became a symbol of tropical escapes, offering a cool, fruity, and indulgent treat that’s perfect for summer or beachside fun.
