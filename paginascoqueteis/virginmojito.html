VIRGIN MOJITO

Ingredient Unit Amount
Fresh Lime Juice ML 20
Sugar Syrup Homemade GR 20
Soda Water or Ginger-Ale ML 120
Mint Leaves UN 6
Lime Wedge U 1
Mint Bouquet U 1
Straw U 1
Cracked Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

Mix mint springs with sugar and lime juice and fill the glass with ice. Pour the ginger ale or soda water and Stir.
Garnish: Lime wheel & mint bouquet

STORYTELLING

The Mojito is believed to have roots in an ancient drink consumed by the native Cubans, but it was popularized in Cuba, especially in Havana, where it was refined and became a symbol of local culture. It is said that the famous writer Ernest Hemingway was a big fan of the Mojito, further increasing its popularity by frequenting La Bodeguita del Medio, a famous bar in Havana where the cocktail was served.
