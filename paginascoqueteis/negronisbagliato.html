NEGRONI SBAGLIATO

Ingredient Unit Amount
Aperitif Bitter ML 30
Sweet Red Vermouth ML 30
Prosecco ML 60
Orange Wheel U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients directly into chilled old fashioned glass filled with ice.Stir gently.
Garnish: Orange Wheel

STORYTELLING

The Negroni Sbagliato was created in Milan, Italy, in the 1970s. The story goes that a bartender at Bar Basso accidentally swapped gin for Prosecco while making a Negroni, and the resulting drink became an instant hit. "Sbagliato" means "mistaken" or "wrong" in Italian, referring to the happy accident that led to the creation of this sparkling twist on the classic Negroni. The Negroni Sbagliato quickly became a popular alternative, offering a lighter, effervescent take on the original.
