MATCHALICIOUS

Ingredient Unit Amount
Matcha tea ML 20
Fresh Lemon Juice ML 20
Pepper mint Kombucha ML 90
Lemon Wheel U 1
Mint Bouquet U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

Dissolve the matcha in hot water and set aside. In a previously chilled highball filled with ice, add all the ingredients and stir.
Garnish: Lemon wheel & mint bouquet

STORYTELLING

Matcha's history dates back to China during the Tang Dynasty (618–907 AD), where powdered tea was consumed by the elite. It was introduced to Japan in the 12th century by Buddhist monks, particularly by Eisai, who promoted its health benefits. Over time, Japan refined the preparation method into the vibrant green tea powder we know today, becoming central to Japanese tea ceremonies. Matcha grew in popularity worldwide in recent decades for its health benefits and unique flavor.
