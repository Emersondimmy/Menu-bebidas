VIRGIN COLADA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Coconut Puree ML 30
Pineapple Juice ML 120
Pineapple Slice U 1
Marrachino Cherry U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Shaker

Glass: Hurricane

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into shaker filled with ice. Shake briskly for few seconds. Strain into chilled hurricane glass.
Garnish: Pineapple slice & marrachino cherry

STORYTELLING

The Virgin Piña Colada is a non-alcoholic version of the Piña Colada, created after the original Piña Colada gained popularity. It removes the rum, making it a refreshing, family-friendly option. While the exact date of its creation is unclear, the Virgin Piña Colada likely emerged as the demand for non-alcoholic drinks grew alongside the original Piña Colada's success.
