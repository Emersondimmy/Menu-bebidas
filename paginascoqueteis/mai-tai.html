MAI-TAI

Ingredient Unit Amount
White Rum ML 30
Dark Rum ML 30
Orgeat Syrup ML 15
Triple-Sec Liqueur ML 15
Fresh Lemon Juice ML 30
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 0.75
Pineapple U 1
Mint Bouquet U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Shaker

Glass: Highball or Double Rocks

PREPARATION

Add all ingredients except the dark rum into a shaker with ice. Shake and pour into a double rocks glass or an highball glass and pour the dark rum on top
Garnish: Pineapple slice & mint bouquet

STORYTELLING

The exact origins of the Mai Tai are disputed, with two bartenders famously claiming to have invented it. One version credits Victor "Trader Vic" Bergeron, who allegedly created the cocktail at his Trader Vic’s restaurant in Oakland, California, in the 1940s. The other claims it was invented by Don the Beachcomber (aka Donn Beach) at his tiki bar in Hollywood in the 1930s. Both versions have their own dedicated following, but the drink’s popularity soared in the 1940s and 1950s, becoming a symbol of the tiki cocktail movement in the United States.
