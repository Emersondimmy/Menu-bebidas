LONG ISLAND ICED TEA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Fresh Lemon Juice ML 30
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 30
Vodka ML 15
Tequila ML 15
White Rum ML 15
Gin ML 15
Triple-Sec Liqueur ML 15
Coca Cola ML 20
Lemon Slice or Wheel U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball or Sling

PREPARATION

Add all ingredients into highball glass filled with ice. Top with cola.
Garnish: Lemon Slice or Wheel

STORYTELLING

The Long Island Iced Tea was created in 1972 by bartender John Coltharp (sometimes credited to Robert "Rosebud" Butt), who was competing in a contest to create a new mixed drink using triple sec. The goal of the competition was to create a drink that would sell well in a bar, and Coltharp's invention was meant to showcase the versatility of mixed liquors while mimickig the taste of iced tea.
