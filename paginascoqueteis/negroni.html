NEGRONI

Ingredient Unit Amount
Gin ML 30
Aperitif Bitter ML 30
Sweet Red Vermouth ML 30
Orange Zest ou Slice U 1
Ice Cubes or Ice Block

Method: Built

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients directly into chilled old fashioned glass filled with ice.Stir gently.
Garnish: Orange zest or slice

STORYTELLING

The Negroni was created in Florence in 1919, when Count Camillo Negroni asked a bartender to modify his usual drink, the Americano, by replacing soda water with gin. The new concoction quickly became popular, and the cocktail was soon named after the Count. With its bold combination of Campari, vermouth, and gin, the Negroni became a classic of Italian cocktail culture and is now enjoyed worldwide for its distinct, bittersweet flavor.
