BELLINI

Ingredient Unit Amount
White Peach Puree ML 50
Prosecco ML 100

Method: Stir

Glass: Flute

PREPARATION

Pour peach puree into the mixing glass with ice, add the Prosecco wine. Stir gently and pour in a chilled flute glass.
Garnish: N/A.

STORYTELLING

The Bellini cocktail was invented in the 1940s by bartender Giuseppe Cipriani at Harry’s Bar in Venice, Italy. Cipriani created the drink by mixing Prosecco with fresh peach puree, inspired by the soft pink color of a painting by Venetian artist Giovanni Bellini. The cocktail quickly became popular, especially among celebrities and tourists, and remains a classic choice for celebrations, known for its light, refreshing taste and elegant simplicity.
