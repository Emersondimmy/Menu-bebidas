GRASSHOPPER

Ingredient Unit Amount
Green Mint Liqueur ML 20
White Cacau Liqueur ML 20
Creams ML 20
Cinnamon Power GR 0.50

Method: Shaker

Glass: Cocktail

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into shaker filled with ice. Shake briskly for few seconds. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.
Garnish: Cinnamon power on top

STORYTELLING

The Grasshopper is a classic, creamy cocktail that was first created in New Orleans in the 1920s. It combines green crème de menthe, white crème de cacao, and cream to create a sweet, minty, and indulgent drink with a smooth texture. The vibrant green color and refreshing mint flavor make it a visually striking and flavorful choice for those who enjoy dessert-like cocktails. The Grasshopper was popularized in the mid-20th century and has remained a favorite for those looking for a decadent, after-dinner drink. Often served in a chilled martini glass, it offers a fun, refreshing twist on traditional creamy cocktails.
