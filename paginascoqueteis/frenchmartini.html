FRENCH MARTINI

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vodka ML 50
Pineapple Juice ML 20
Raspberry Liqueur ML 20
Pineapple Slice U 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Cocktail

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker, shake well with ice, strain into chilled cocktail glass.
Garnish: Pineapple Slice.

STORYTELLING

The French Martini was created in the 1980s in New York, although it’s inspired by the chic and glamorous essence of French culture. It gained popularity quickly for its sweet, fruity flavor and vibrant color, combining smooth vodka, raspberry liqueur (often Chambord), and pineapple juice. The drink became a staple in the cocktail scene, especially among those looking for something more playful yet sophisticated, offering a modern twist on traditional martinis. With its elegant appearance and deliciously balanced taste, the French Martini embodies a taste of Parisian sophistication in a glass.
