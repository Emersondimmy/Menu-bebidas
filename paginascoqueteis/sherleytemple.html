SHIRLEY TEMPLE

Ingredient Unit Amount
Ginger Ale ML 150
Grenadine Syrup ML 20
Marrachino Cherry U 2
Lime Wheel U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Highball

PREPARATION

Build all ingredients in a highball glass filled with ice.
Garnish: Lime Wheel and marrachino cherry

STORYTELLING

The Shirley Temple is a classic non-alcoholic cocktail made with ginger ale (or sometimes lemon-lime soda), grenadine, and garnished with a maraschino cherry. It’s a sweet, fruity drink, named after the famous child actress Shirley Temple, who was known for her cheerful personality and popularity in the 1930s. The drink was created as a fun, kid-friendly alternative to cocktails at adult events, and it quickly became a favorite for children.
