HUGO SPRITZ

Ingredient Unit Amount
Liqueur Giffard Premium Fleur
de Sureau Sauvage 		ML 45
Prosecco ML 60
Soda Water ML 60
Lime Wedge or Wheel U 1
Mint Bouquet U 1
Straw U 1
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Wine or Spritz

PREPARATION

Build all ingredients into a wine glass filled with ice. Stir gently.
Garnish: Lime wedge or wheel and mint bouquet

STORYTELLING

The Hugo Spritz was created in the early 2000s by bartender Roland Gruber in the Trentino-Alto Adige region of northern Italy. It quickly gained popularity for its refreshing and floral flavor, becoming a favorite aperitif in Italy and spreading internationally. Its light and easy-to-drink nature made it perfect for warm weather and casual gatherings.
