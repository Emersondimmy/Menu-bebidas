PORN STAR MARTINI

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vanilla Vodka ML 40
Passion Fruit Liqueur ML 20
Passion Fruit Puree ML 20
Prosecco ML 50
Vanilla Syrup ML 15
Fresh Lime Juice ML 15
Half Passion Fruit U 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Cocktail or Coupe

International Day: 6 SEP

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker, shake well with ice, double strain into a large chilled cocktail glass. Accompany with a shot of Prosecco.
Garnish: Half passionfruit

STORYTELLING

In the early 2000s, bartender Douglas Ankrah wanted to create a cocktail that captured the energy of London’s nightlife—bold, playful, and unforgettable. Inspired by the vibrant party scene, he crafted the Porn Star Martini, a drink that was as indulgent as it was fun. With a cheeky name and a tropical, refreshing taste, the cocktail quickly became a hit, embodying the glamour and excitement of the night. It was a drink that demanded attention and delivered on flavor.
