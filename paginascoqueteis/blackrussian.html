BLACK RUSSIAN

Ingredient Unit Amount
Vodka ML 50
Coffee Liqueur ML 20
Cubed Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Old fashion

PREPARATION

Pour the ingredients into the old fashioned glass filled with ice cubes. Stir gently.
Garnish: N/A.
NOTE: WHITE RUSSIAN – Float fresh cream on the top.

STORYTELLING

The Black Russian cocktail was created in 1949 by bartender Gustave Tops at the Hotel Metropole in Brussels, Belgium. The drink was originally made with just two ingredients: vodka and coffee liqueur. It was named "Black" due to the dark color of the coffee liqueur, and "Russian" as a nod to the vodka base. The cocktail quickly became popular for its simple yet bold flavor, and over time, it evolved into variations like the *White Russian* by adding cream.
