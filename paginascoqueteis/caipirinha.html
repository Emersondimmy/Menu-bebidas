CAIPIRINHA

Ingredient Unit Amount
Cachaça ML 60
White Sugar GR 20
Lime Wedges U 3
Straw U 1
Lime Wheel U 1
Cracked Ice

Method: Built

Glass: Old Fashion

PREPARATION

Place lime and sugar into old fashioned glass and muddle gently. Fill the glass with cracked ice and add cachaça. Stir gently.
Garnish: Lime Wheel.

STORYTELLING

The Caipirinha is Brazil's national cocktail, originating in the early 20th century. Made with cachaça, sugar, lime, and ice, it was initially a medicinal drink but quickly became a beloved refreshment, embodying the vibrant and tropical spirit of Brazil.
