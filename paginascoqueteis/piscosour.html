PISCO SOUR

Ingredient Unit Amount
Pisco ML 60
Fresh Lemon Juice ML 30
Sugar Syrup Homemade ML 20
Vegan Foam ML 1
Angostura Biter ML 1

Method: Shaker

Glass: Sour

International Day:1 FEB

PREPARATION

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a chilled sour glass. If requested, serve in a old fashion glass filled with ice.
Garnish: 3 angostura bitter drops on top

STORYTELLING

The Pisco Sour is a classic cocktail from Peru, though it’s also popular in Chile, where both countries claim its origin. It was first created in the early 20th century, with one story crediting bartender Victor Vaughen Morris, who worked in Lima, Peru, in the 1920s. He combined pisco (a local brandy), lemon juice, sugar, egg white, and bitters to create a refreshing and frothy drink. The Pisco Sour has become a symbol of Peruvian culture and is enjoyed in both countries, especially as a celebratory drink.
