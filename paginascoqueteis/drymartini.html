DRY MARTINI

Ingredient Unit Amount
Gin ML 60
Dry Vermute ML 10
Lemon Zest U 1
Olives U 2

Method: Stir

Glass: Cocktail

PREPARATION

Pour all ingredients into mixing glass with ice cubes. Stir well. Strain into chilled martini cocktail glass.
Garnish: Lemon zest.
Check with the customer if they like olives, If yes, put 2 skewered olives.

STORYTELLING

"Indulge in the timeless sophistication of the Dry Martini, a cocktail that exudes class with its perfect balance of smooth gin and crisp dry vermouth. Served chilled and garnished with a lemon twist or olive, it’s the drink that never goes out of style—ideal for those who appreciate elegance in every sip."
